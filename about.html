About Me

My journey in the music industry has equipped me with a keen eye for detail, a deep understanding of complex systems, and the ability to adapt quickly to new technologies. These skills seamlessly translate into my role as a data analyst, where I am passionate about leveraging data to uncover actionable insights and solve real-world problems.

My key skills include:
• Extracting meaningful insights from large amounts of data using Microsoft Excel, Python and SQL.
• Identifying trends and making predictions using machine learning and deep learning techniques in Python.
• Creating compelling visuals and dashboards using Tableau.
